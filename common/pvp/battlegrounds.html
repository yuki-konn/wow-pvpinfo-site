Battlegrounds

Battlegrounds are an instance where two teams compete for experience, honor, and sometimes rating. Usually battlegrounds are Alliance vs Horde. Except in rated battlegrounds the same faction teams can compete against eachother.

Battleground Types

Resource Race

Name: Arathi Basin

Battleground size: 15 vs 15

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Hold 3 or more nodes.


Name: Eye of the Storm

Battleground size: 15 vs 15

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Hold 3 or more nodes. Or hold one node and capture flag.


Name: Battle for Gilneas

Battleground size: 10 vs 10

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Hold 2 or more nodes.


Name: Silvershard Mines

Battleground size: 10 vs 10

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Hold 2 or more carts. Or zerg the nearest cart.


Name: Temple of Kotmogu

Battleground size: 10 vs 10

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Grab orbs then jump into the middle. Stay alive and hold the orb as long as possible. Kill enemy orb carriers. Best to hold 3 or more orbs and control middle.


Name: Deepwind Gorge

Battleground size: 15 vs 15

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Hold 3 or more nodes.


Capture the Flag

Name: Warsong Gulch

Battleground size: 10 vs 10

Win Condition: Capture 3 flags

Strategy: Fight enemy team in the middle at the start. Kill enemy group then escort flag carrier to enemy flag then escort flag carrier back to base. Send kill squad to kill enemy flag carrier.


Name: Twin Peaks

Battleground size: 10 vs 10

Win Condition: Capture 3 flags

Strategy: Fight enemy team in the middle at the start. Kill enemy group then escort flag carrier to enemy flag then escort flag carrier back to base. Send kill squad to kill enemy flag carrier.


Warfare

Name: Alterac Valley

Battleground size: 40 vs 40

Win Condition: Burn 6 towers and kill enemy leader.

Strategy: Hold 3 or more nodes.


Name: Isle of Conquest

Battleground size: 40 vs 40

Win Condition: 1500 points

Strategy: Destroy Gates and kill enemy leader.
