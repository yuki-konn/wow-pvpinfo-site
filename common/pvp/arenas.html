Arenas

Arenas are 2 vs 2 or 3 vs 3 small team death matches. Which ever team is the last one standing is the winner.

Arena Titles/Rating

Arena rating is earned by winning arenas. The higher rating you are the less rating you get and the more rating you lose. The highest title you can get in arenas is Gladiator.
