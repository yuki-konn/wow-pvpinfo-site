Player vs Player

Player vs Player also known as PvP is part of the game where players fight eachother. The main goal of pvp differs based on the type of pvp you are doing. The 4 types of pvp are arenas, battleground, world pvp, and dueling. There are also the rated version of arenas and battlegrounds.

Honor Points

Honor is a type of currency used to purchase pvp gear at the pvp vendors. Honor is earned through getting honorable kills. The types of pvp honor can be earned in are arenas, battlegrounds, and world pvp.

Rating

Rating can only be earned through rated pvp which includes arenas, and rated battlegrounds.
